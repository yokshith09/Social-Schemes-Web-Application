FORTHEPEOPLE is an e-Marketplace for Govt. schemes and services. Using forthepeople you can find various government schemes, check your eligibility, and apply for the schemes.


ForThePeople will reduce the time and effort of citizens in searching for appropriate government schemes.The easy user interface of ForThePeople helps common people a lot in discovering and applying for schemes.


For now, the platform shall direct you to the application page of the scheme of your choice.It is envisaged that in the upcoming phases, ForThePeople shall have the feature to apply for schemes / services from within the platform/ app.


ForThePeople provides details on the eligibility criteria, application process, and benefits being offered under that scheme. ForThePeople also answers the Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs) about the scheme.
