About us



Our Vision

Our aspiration is to simplify the lives of citizens.

Our Mission

Our commitment is to enhance the accessibility and efficiency of government services. We endeavor to optimize the interface between citizens and government schemes, minimizing the time and effort required to discover and utilize available benefits.

FORTHEPEOPLE is a mirror image of the MyScholar website, which serves as a nationwide hub for comprehensive exploration and accessibility to educational grants and scholarships.

FORTHEPEOPLE introduces a pioneering, technology-driven approach to unraveling scholarship details tailored to individual eligibility criteria.

The platform helps the citizen to find the right Government schemes for them. It also guides on how to apply for different Government schemes. Thus no need to visit multiple Government websites.

FORTHEPEOPLE is conceived, supervised, and executed by a dedicated group of students from Class E at Amrita University.
