#INDIAN SCHEMES

FOR THE PEOPLE

This website provides you information of different schemes initiated by the Indian government for the benefit of people and guides you on how to register for the specific schemes

In India, a significant number of people are unaware of government schemes meant to support them, and many who are aware face barriers to access. The complexity of the scheme landscape, coupled with bureaucratic hurdles and concerns about corruption, deters eligible individuals from applying. Efforts to simplify processes, improve communication are needed to ensure equitable access to these benefits and foster socio-economic development for all.
